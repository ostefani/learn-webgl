Basic Phong Shading

The Phong shading model calculates lighting per-fragment based on:

  • Ambient: Constant light from all directions
  • Diffuse: Light based on surface angle to light
  • Specular: Reflections based on viewing angle

This is a classic lighting model for basic 3D scenes.

Normal mapping adds the illusion of fine surface detail without adding more geometry.

  • Tangent Space: The normals are encoded relative to the surface
  • Detail Normals: The texture provides high-resolution normal information
  • Perception of Depth: Lighting responds to surface details

Compare with basic shading to see how surface details affect light reflection.

Physically-Based Rendering (PBR) simulates how light interacts with materials in the real world:

  • Metalness: How metal-like the material is
  • Roughness: How rough or smooth the surface is
  • Energy Conservation: Light energy is preserved

This implementation uses a simplified microfacet BRDF model.
